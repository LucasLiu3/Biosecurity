1. Stump swab (all year round): metsulfuron-methyl 600g/kg (5g/L) or a product containing 200g/litre 2,4-D plus 100g/litre dicamba (200ml/L) or dicamba 50g/L (400ml/L). Add penetrant to all mixes. Dispose of all cut stems at a refuse transfer station, or burn or bury deeply.
2. Spray (regrowth): glyphosate (150ml/15L + penetrant (knapsack) or 1L/100L + penetrant (spraygun)) or metsulfuron-methyl 600g/kg (5g/10L + penetrant (knapsack) or 40g/100L + penetrant (spraygun)) or a product containing 200g 2,4-D+100g dicamba/L (120ml/L) or dicamba 50g/L (24ml/L).
